ABC POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE — STUDENT HANDBOOK (2026 EDITION)

1. ABOUT THE COLLEGE
ABC Polytechnic College is a state-affiliated technical institution offering
three-year diploma programmes in Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Computer, and
Electronics Engineering. The campus is located at 42 Industrial Road and is
open to students from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM on working days.

2. ACADEMIC CALENDAR
The academic year is divided into two semesters. The odd semester runs from
July to November and the even semester runs from January to May. December and
June are reserved for examinations, results, and semester breaks.

3. ATTENDANCE POLICY
Attendance is compulsory for all theory and practical classes. A minimum of
75% attendance is required to be eligible to appear for the end-semester
examination. Students whose attendance falls between 65% and 75% may apply for
condonation by paying a fee and submitting a valid medical certificate.
Students with attendance below 65% will be detained and must repeat the
semester. Attendance is recorded separately for each subject.

4. LIBRARY
The central library is located in Block B, Ground Floor. The library opens at
9:00 AM and closes at 6:00 PM on working days, and remains open until 1:00 PM
on Saturdays. Each student may borrow up to four books at a time for a period
of fourteen days. A late return fine of five rupees per day per book applies.
Reference books and journals may be used inside the library only and cannot be
borrowed. Silence must be maintained at all times.

5. HOSTEL RULES
The college provides separate hostels for male and female students. The hostel
gates close at 10:00 PM every night, and all residents must sign the entry
register on return. Visitors are allowed only in the visitor's lounge between
4:00 PM and 6:00 PM. Cooking inside hostel rooms is strictly prohibited.
Ragging in any form is a criminal offence and will lead to immediate expulsion.

6. EXAMINATION RULES
Students must carry their college identity card and hall ticket to every
examination. Entry to the examination hall is not permitted after 30 minutes
from the start of the exam. The use of mobile phones, smart watches, or any
electronic device in the examination hall is strictly forbidden and will be
treated as malpractice. A student found copying will have that paper cancelled.
The pass mark for each subject is 40% in theory and 50% in practical.

7. FEES AND PAYMENTS
The tuition fee for the academic year must be paid in two installments, before
the start of each semester. A late payment surcharge of two hundred rupees is
charged after the due date. Fee receipts must be preserved and produced when
collecting the hall ticket. Scholarships are available for students from
economically weaker sections; applications are submitted to the office in July.

8. ADMINISTRATION AND OFFICES
The Principal's office is located in Room 101 on the first floor of the
Administrative Block, and is open from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. The Examination
Cell is in Room 105. The Accounts Office, where fees are paid, is in Room 110.
For any grievance, students may meet the Student Welfare Officer in Room 112.

9. DRESS CODE AND CONDUCT
Students must wear the prescribed uniform on Mondays and during all practical
sessions and industrial visits. Identity cards must be worn visibly on campus
at all times. Smoking, consumption of alcohol, and the use of any prohibited
substance on campus are strictly forbidden and will lead to disciplinary action.

10. CONTACT
The college office can be reached at 0123-456789 during working hours, or by
email at office@abcpolytechnic.edu. The college website lists all circulars,
the academic calendar, and examination schedules.
