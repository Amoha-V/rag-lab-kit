1. DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW
The Computer Engineering Department is located in Block C. The Head of
Department (HOD) sits in Room 301. The department runs four computer
laboratories: the Programming Lab, the Networking Lab, the Hardware Lab, and
the Project Lab.

2. LABORATORY TIMINGS
All computer labs are open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM on working days. The Project
Lab stays open until 7:00 PM for final-year students working on their projects.
Students must enter their login time in the lab register and log out before
leaving. Eating and drinking inside the labs is not allowed.

3. PROGRAMMING SUBJECTS
First-year students study C programming. Second-year students study Java and
Data Structures. Third-year students study Python, Web Development, and a course
on Databases. Each programming subject has a weekly three-hour practical.

4. PROJECT REQUIREMENTS
Every final-year student must complete a major project in a team of up to four
members. The project proposal is due in August, the mid-review is in November,
and the final demonstration is in April. Each team is assigned a faculty guide.
A project report of at least forty pages must be submitted in printed and soft
copy form.

5. LAB EQUIPMENT
The Hardware Lab contains forty desktop computers, networking switches, and
microcontroller kits. Students must report any damaged equipment to the lab
assistant immediately. Deliberate damage to equipment will be charged to the
student responsible.

6. INTERNSHIP
Students are required to complete a six-week industrial internship after the
fourth semester. The internship must be approved by the HOD in advance, and an
internship report and certificate must be submitted within two weeks of
returning to college.

7. CONTACT
The department office is in Room 302 and is open from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
Queries can be emailed to computer.dept@abcpolytechnic.edu.
